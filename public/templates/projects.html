Projects for Clients

Wordpress PHP MySQL
A support center for employees of Delamar Hospitality Group. In before, employees need to exchange emails whenever they need to resolve issues or clients' requessts. To prevent the mailbox from getting overwhelemed, I created this portal that allows employees to send out requests by filling out a quick form. It will record those requests. The Support department can easily keep track and resolve these requests, and to announce any progresses accordingly.
